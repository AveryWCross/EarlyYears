Our services include:

  • Resources and information on behavioral and developmental issues.
  • Individualized coaching using current research-based techniques and time-tested problem-solving strategies.
  • Small-group workshops with other parents.
  • Support during developmental assessment, medical and counseling services.
  • Support through the foster care and adoption process.

Topics we address:

  • Crying, colic and getting in tune
  • Sleeping and soothing
  • Breast and bottle feeding
  • Toilet learning
  • Mealtimes and nutrition
  • Sharing, turn-taking, siblings and peer relationships
  • Aggresion and acting out
  • Dependence/independence, separation anxiety, adjusting to childcare or preschool
  • Cognition, language development and Baby Signs
  • Optimizing parent-child and parent-parent communication
